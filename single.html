About IGS-SC

The Interdisciplinary Graduate School Student Club (IGS–SC) is the official student club, supported by the IGS and all the institutions under its umbrella, and affiliated to NTU Graduate Student Council. One of the most active and dynamic student clubs of NTU, IGS-SC began its activities in January 2013 with a handful of members and has been steadily growing, both in number and activity. The club works towards strengthening the interpersonal and professional interaction among the IGS students, to enrich their experiences during PhD and beyond.



Our mission and vision

1. Promoting interdisciplinary knowledge and skill exchange.
2. Development of a cohesive culture within the IGS student community.
3. Acting as a bridge of communication between the students and IGS officials.



IGS student club constitution

The IGS-SC constitution was created in early 2014 and laid the foundation for a strong core of dedicated members. All IGS students are automatically constituent members of the club and are invited to vote on elections, and to participate in any and all events organised by the club.
